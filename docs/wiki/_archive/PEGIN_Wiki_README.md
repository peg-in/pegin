## 💡 Key Insights Summary

### The Core Problem PEGIN Solves
- **Current state:** Your login belongs to your company (Okta, Azure)
- **PEGIN solution:** You own your identity on blockchain

### The Permission Problem PEGIN Solves
- **Current state:** Permissions take 3-7 days
- **PEGIN solution:** One-click approvals (< 2 minutes)

### The Business Value
- **Cost savings:** $690K/year per 10K employees
- **Time savings:** 8 months faster development
- **Cost reduction:** 60% cheaper than Okta

### Why It Wins
- Free (open source) vs $2-5/user for Okta
- User-owned (survives company death) vs company-owned
- Instant permissions (DIG Network) vs slow (LDAP)
- Immutable audit (blockchain) vs deletable logs
